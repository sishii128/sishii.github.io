BEST LEMONADE EVER

Twenty servings ready in just under five hours

  • 1¾ c white sugar
  • 8 c water
  • 1½ c fresh lemon juice
  1. In a small saucepan, combine sugar and 1 cup of water
  2. Bring the saucepan to a boil and stir until sugar is completely disolved.
  3. Remove seeds from the hand squeezed lemon juice, but leave most of the pulp.
  4. In a pitcher, stir together chilled syrup, lemon juice and remaining 7 cups of water.
  5. Mix together to pale yellow color. Cool in a refrigerator
  6. Serve in a glass cup with a couple of ice. Best if not used for sibling rivalry and lemonade stand competitions.
